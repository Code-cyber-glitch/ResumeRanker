Data Scientist – AI & Analytics Team

We are looking for a passionate Data Scientist to join our growing AI & Analytics team. The ideal candidate will turn complex data into actionable insights, build predictive models, and contribute to intelligent decision-making across the organization.

Key Responsibilities

Analyze structured and unstructured datasets to uncover patterns and trends

Build and deploy machine learning models for prediction, classification, and recommendation tasks

Perform data cleaning, feature engineering, and exploratory data analysis (EDA)

Work with stakeholders to translate business problems into data science solutions

Develop dashboards and visualizations to communicate insights clearly

Optimize model performance and ensure scalability in production environments

Required Skills

Strong programming skills in Python or R

Experience with Machine Learning, NLP, or Deep Learning techniques

Hands-on knowledge of libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, or PyTorch

Proficiency in SQL and data querying

Understanding of statistics, probability, and model evaluation metrics

Experience with data visualization tools like Matplotlib, Seaborn, or Tableau

Preferred Qualifications

Experience working with real-world datasets and messy data

Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS / GCP / Azure)

Familiarity with Git, Docker, or MLOps workflows

Prior internship/project experience in data science or analytics

Education

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, or related field.

What We Offer

Opportunity to work on real AI-driven products

Learning budget and mentorship from senior data scientists
